Effective date: June 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how UpGrind ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email via Firebase Authentication. Alternatively, you may use Google Sign-In to create or log in to your account.
  • App Data: We securely store your tasks, XP progress, and preferences in Firebase.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies to improve your experience (e.g., remembering settings and saving login sessions).

How We Use Your Information

  • To save your tasks, XP, and timer progress.
  • To personalize your experience.
  • To improve our app and fix bugs.

Ad Services

If we serve ads in the future, third-party providers (such as Google AdSense or Adsterra) may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can manage ad personalization through your browser settings.

Data Security

All data is stored securely using Firebase. We do not share, sell, or misuse your data.

Your Rights

  • You can choose to delete your account or data at your own discretion in the app's settings section.
  • Your account and the data linked to it gets immediately deleted from firebase.

Changes

We may update this policy. You will be notified via the app when we do.
